Big One and Little One

Daughterly wisdom | Brutal wit | Over a decade of tweets

by Aaron R. Dunone

Big One and Little One is a curated collection of remarks, observations and moments gathered quietly over more than a decade of everyday family life. Funny, strange and occasionally disarming, it sits somewhere between a diary and a domestic archive - a record of ordinary things said out loud and never meant to be preserved, but tragic to lose.
